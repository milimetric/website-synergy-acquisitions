Sell-side Advisory

We know that every business brings different obstacles to the table and through experience we have embraced the concept of recognizing and understanding those strengths and weaknesses. Below is a general outline and timeline of a traditional transaction cycle.

  • Business Valuation
    • Identify strategic strengths and weaknesses
    • Analyze historical and projected financial performance
  • Preparation of the marketing package
    • Describes the company, its products, culture and employees.
    • Includes a thorough financial analysis
  • Identify Potential Buyers
    • Includes individuals, corporate buyers and equity funds.
  • Negotiate Letter of Intent
    • Ensure that all of the essential terms are included
  • Manage Closing Process
    • Coordinate the Purchase-Sale Agreement, other legal requirements, accounting, tax, any financial introductions to ensure all interests are attended to a completed in a timely manner.
