Sell-side Advisory

While every business we have come across is uniquely valuable and comes with many strengths that could lead to continued growth, it also contains natural weaknesses that may need to be improved on prior to a potential transaction. Our experienced team can walk the current management/ownership through the process of preparing the company for sale as well as the transaction process itself. Below is a general outline and timeline of a traditional transaction cycle.

  • Business Valuation
    • Identifies strategic strengths and weaknesses.
    • Analyzes historical and projected financial performance.
  • Preparation of the Confidential Marketing Package
    • Gives a company overview, detail its products, culture, target market, demographics and employees.
    • Includes a thorough historical financial analysis.
  • Identification of Potential Buyers
    • Includes individuals, corporate buyers and equity firms.
  • Negotiation of the Letter of Intent
    • Ensures that all of the essential terms are included and that the price is agreeable.
  • Management of the Closing Process
    • Coordinates the Purchase-Sale Agreement, other legal requirements as well as a comprehensive due diligence process to ensure all parties are satisfied.
