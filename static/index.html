Synergy Acquisitions is a boutique investment banking firm that specializes in mergers and acquisitions advisory for Main Street and Lower Market clients. After 7 years in the industry, it became apparent that Capital Firms were investing in companies that were already for sale, rather than making ideal companies available.

Synergy Acquisitions was built to service Equity Firms by helping them find their ideal company in their ideal industry which meets most, if not all, of their investment criteria. By combining transaction expertise with industry-specific knowledge, we have established a process typically only available from large investment banking firms.
